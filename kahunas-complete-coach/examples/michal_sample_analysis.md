## 🧬 ZOE-Style Analysis (Metabolic Health)

**Finding:** 4 litres daily fluid intake  
**Why it matters:** Optimal hydration supports kidney function, glucose clearance, nutrient transport, and metabolic efficiency.  
**Action:** Maintain current intake. Consider adding pinch of sea salt to one litre for electrolyte balance.  
**Target:** Daily 3.5-4.5L maintained

### 💡 Early Morning Hunger Pattern
**Finding:** Hungriest at 6am  
**Why it matters:** Early waking hunger may indicate overnight hypoglycemia or inadequate evening protein. ZOE research shows this affects glucose variability.  
**Action:** Add 20-30g casein or Greek yogurt 1 hour before bed. Slow-digesting protein stabilizes overnight blood sugar.  
**Target:** Shift hunger timing to 8am+ over 2 weeks

### 💡 Moderate Caffeine Intake
**Finding:** 3-4 coffees daily  
**Why it matters:** Caffeine can blunt insulin sensitivity and affect sleep architecture if consumed late.  
**Action:** Set caffeine cutoff at 2pm. Consider reducing to 2-3 or switching afternoon coffee to decaf.  
**Target:** 100% caffeine compliance before 2pm

### 🔥 Work Event Impact
**Finding:** Off-plan due to work events and using up ingredients  
**Why it matters:** Unpredictable meals increase glucose variability. ZOE data shows high variability linked to inflammation and poor metabolic outcomes.  
**Action:** Pre-plan work event meals with protein-first strategy. Order protein + vegetables before starch.  
**Target:** 90%+ meal tracking compliance next week
